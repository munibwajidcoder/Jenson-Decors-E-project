The Artisan's Story

Each Obsidian piece begins in our forge, where cold-rolled steel is heated and hammered by hand to create the signature monolithic frame. Our master glass-smiths then apply a proprietary charcoal tint that allows light to penetrate while maintaining a deep, void-like aesthetic when extinguished. It is not just a mirror—it is a study in spatial perception.
